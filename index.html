Introduction to Chameleon

Pre-Alpha Phase

Chameleon is currently in its Pre-Alpha stage. The community has recently voted and finalized both the name and mascot. We are officially established as Chameleon, A Privacy Network. Our next steps involve laying the foundation for a vibrant community and rebuilding from the remnants of Incognito Chain, the now-defunct privacy blockchain from which Chameleon was forked.
