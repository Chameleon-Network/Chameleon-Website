Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Information you provide to us directly, such as your name, email address, or other contact details when you register, subscribe, or contact us.

  • Usage Data: Non-personal information collected automatically, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing activity on our site.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services.
  • To respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • To analyze website usage and improve functionality.

Self-Hosted Analytics and Metrics

We prioritize your privacy by using self-hosted analytics and metrics tools. These tools ensure your data remains under our control and is not shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

We minimize reliance on third-party services. However, we use Cloudflare for DDoS protection and web performance optimization. Cloudflare's privacy policy can be found here.


Additionally, we utilize CrowdSec, a collaborative cybersecurity tool, to identify and mitigate malicious IP addresses. CrowdSec analyzes behavior patterns and works with a community-driven database to enhance security and protect our services from potential threats. This tool ensures a safer browsing experience for all our users.

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption to safeguard data during transmission.
  • Robust access controls to limit unauthorized access to sensitive information.
  • Advanced threat detection and prevention mechanisms to block malicious activities and enhance system security.
  • Continuous monitoring and timely application of updates to maintain optimal protection.
  • Comprehensive safeguards to mitigate risks associated with unauthorized access or attacks.

Your Rights

  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by sending a message to our support staff.
