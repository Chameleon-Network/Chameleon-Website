What is CHML?

CHML is the native token of the Chameleon Network with a fixed supply of 100 million. It serves multiple functions:

  • Staking: Validators (V-Nodes/P-Nodes) must stake CHML to participate in consensus and earn rewards.
  • Transaction Fees: CHML is used to pay network fees (e.g., for transactions and shielding/unshielding).
  • Governance: CHML holders can vote on network proposals (e.g., roadmap decisions, feature prioritization).
  • Liquidity Mining: Incentivize liquidity providers on pDEX with CHML rewards.

CHML Tokenomics Overview

  • Total Supply: 100,000,000 CHML (fixed supply, no inflation).
  • The total supply is capped to create scarcity and preserve value over time.
  • No additional minting will occur after the initial supply is distributed.
